Build a full stack app, get a stack of pancakes!

What is pancakes?

Pancakes is a YSWS where you build a full stack app by going through "stacks", and get rewarded with pancakes!


Stack 1

Here's where you'll begin pancakes! For your first stack of pancakes, you'll be building a frontend with HTML, CSS, and JS!


Stack 2

For your next stack of pancakes, you'll build a backend in Python with Flask and learn about databases!


Stack 3

Finally, it's time for you to add toppings to your pancakes by combining both ends and customizing your app!


FAQ

A 20$ food grant along with a sticker!

If you are participating as part of a workshop, we will ship out your rewards to the organizers of the workshop, at which point they will distribute it to you, with the sticker coming at Stack 1 and the grant at Stack 3! (your organizer may choose to recieve a 5$ grant at stack 1 as well for snacks!) Otherwise, once you submit your final app, we will ship your rewards to you!

If you are participating as part of a workshop, submit after each stack! Otherwise, submit when you are done with all three stacks.

You will need to track your time on hackatime.hackclub.com, or you will not be able to submit!
